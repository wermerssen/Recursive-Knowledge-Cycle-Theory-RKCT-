Recursive Knowledge Cycle Theory (RKCT)

The Recursive Knowledge Cycle Theory (RKCT) is a structural model describing how knowledge evolves through recursive refinement, abstraction, and integration. This page provides access to the official OSF project and the historical development of the RRK hypothesis.
