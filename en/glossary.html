Glossary of Key Terms

Knowledge System
A structured set of patterns, processes, and relations that generate, maintain, and transform knowledge.
Transition Sequence
The six‑phase cycle through which knowledge systems reorganize themselves.
Stabilization
A phase in which structures become reproducible and resistant to change.
Breakdown
The collapse of previously stable structures once tensions exceed a threshold.
Exploratory Expansion
A generative phase in which the system produces a wide range of structural or conceptual variations.
Re‑Consolidation
The integration and stabilization of successful variants.
Recursive Embedding
The embedding of stabilized structures into higher‑order cycles.
