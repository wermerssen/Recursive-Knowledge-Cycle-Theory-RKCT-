Recursive Knowledge Cycle Theory (RKCT)

The Recursive Knowledge Cycle Theory (RKCT) provides a unified framework for understanding how knowledge systems evolve across biological, cultural, technological, and artificial domains. It describes how structures stabilize, accumulate tensions, break down, explore new configurations, and eventually re‑consolidate into higher‑order forms.
