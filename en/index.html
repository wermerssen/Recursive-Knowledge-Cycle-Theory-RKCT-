Recursive Knowledge Cycle Theory (RKCT)

The Recursive Knowledge Cycle Theory (RKCT) describes transition processes in which knowledge systems generate new forms of organization, reproduction, and self‑structuring. It is an open theoretical framework designed to reveal recurring patterns across biological, cultural, and technological domains.
